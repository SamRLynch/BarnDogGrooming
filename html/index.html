ABOUT US

Sylvia founded Barndog Grooming in 2017 after developing a stellar reputation in the grooming business. Prior to this, she attended grooming school while working in facilities around the Southern Maine and New Hampshire area. Her love of animals and study of dog behavior enables her to take compassionate care of your pets. We strive for every pet to fully enjoy their spa day. A place where pets can clean up and unwind.
